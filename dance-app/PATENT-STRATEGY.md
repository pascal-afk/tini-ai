#### 4. **Count-In Timer Preparation System**
**Novelty:** Automated countdown synchronized to musical phrase structure preparing learner for choreography start.

- Calculates preceding bar timing based on BPM

- Learner has exact time to get into starting position
- Synchronized to musical phrasing (typically 4 or 8 counts before start)

**Patent Claim Focus:**
> "An automated preparation system for choreographic instruction comprising a countdown display synchronized to musical phrase structure, providing preparatory count-in beats preceding choreography onset, timed to the musical tempo and phrase boundaries."

**What to File:**
