## üéõÔ∏è Your Tasks (What Still Needs To Be Done)

### **Task 1: Formant Shifting** (CRITICAL)
**Why:** Changes the "throat size" of the AI vocal, making it unrecognizable as AI-generated

**Instructions:**
1. Load `cyprus_sun_vocal_chop.wav` into your DAW
2. Apply **Little AlterBoy** (Soundtoys) or **MAutoPitch** (Melda Production)
3. Settings:
   - **Formant Shift:** -2 to -3 semitones (experiment to find sweet spot)
   - **Pitch:** Keep at 0 (already shifted in pre-processing)
   - **Mix:** 100% wet
4. **Goal:** The vocal should sound like a different singer/character

**Why this wasn't done earlier:** Requires specialized DAW plugins not available in automated processing

---

### **Task 2: Vocal Chop Drop (OPTIONAL BUT RECOMMENDED)**
**Why:** Transforms the vocal from "lead singer" to "sampled instrument" (safer for Content ID)

**Instructions:**
1. Identify the chorus phrase: **"So beautiful"** or similar
2. Slice into individual **vowel sounds only**: "Oh", "Euu", "Ah"
   - Discard consonants (that's where AI artifacts are most audible)
3. Create a new **rhythmic pattern** for the drop section
   - Example rhythm: "Oh - Oh - Euu - Ah - (Rest) - Ah - Euu - Oh"
   - Make it dance-y and syncopated (not just straight quarter notes)
4. Apply **heavy reverb** (room size 0.7, wet 0.4)
5. Apply **sidechain compression** triggered by kick drum
   - Threshold: -20dB, Ratio: 8:1, Attack: 1ms, Release: 150ms

**Style Reference:** Major Lazer, DJ Snake, Marshmello vocal chops

**If time is limited:** Skip this step and just do formant shifting (still effective)

---

### **Task 3: Instrumental Humanization** (OPTIONAL)
**Why:** AI-generated music is perfectly quantized (too robotic)

**Instructions:**
1. **Percussion layering:**
   - Layer **organic foley sounds** over existing snare/clap
   - Suggestions: wood blocks, real handclaps, shakers
   - Volume: 20-30% blend with existing drums
   
2. **Groove/Swing:**
   - Apply a slight **Swing template** (52-55%) to percussion and bass
   - Or manually shift MIDI/audio by 5-10ms on off-beats
   
3. **MIDI Humanization (if you have MIDI access):**
   - Randomize velocity: ¬±5-10%
   - Randomize timing: ¬±5-10ms

**If time is limited:** Skip this step - the vocal treatment is more critical

---

### **Task 4: Structure Enhancement** (OPTIONAL)
**Why:** AI songs often loop the same 4-bar pattern (boring)

**Instructions:**
1. Add a **Bridge section** before the final drop
   - Remove drums and bass
   - Keep only piano + vocal (or minimal elements)
   - Duration: 8 bars (15-20 seconds)
   - Purpose: Creates dynamic contrast and "compositional intent"

**If time is limited:** Skip this step

---

### **Task 5: Final Mastering** (REQUIRED)
**Why:** Ensures broadcast-ready quality for streaming platforms

**Instructions:**
1. **Target Loudness:** -14 LUFS (Spotify/Apple Music standard)
2. **True Peak:** -1.0 dB maximum (avoid clipping)
3. **Stereo Width:** Slight stereo widening on vocals (not too much)
4. **Reference Tracks:** 
   - Kygo - "Firestone"
   - Sigala - "Easy Love"
   - Match the tonal balance and energy

**Export Settings:**
- Format: WAV 44.1kHz / 16-bit
